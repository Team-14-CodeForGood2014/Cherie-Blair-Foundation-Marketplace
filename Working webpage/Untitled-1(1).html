By combining mentoring with technology, the Mentoring Women in Business Programme is pioneering a new way of supporting women entrepreneurs.

 

Many women in developing and emerging markets have the ideas and ambition to become successful entrepreneurs.

 

We want to ensure long-term progress. Our packaged support equips women entrepreneurs with the tools to transition into small and growing businesses, enabling them to increase their customer base.  

Over 93% of mentees who finished the programme by October 2013 were able to achieve a business goal.
